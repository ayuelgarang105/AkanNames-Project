WELCOME TO AKAN-NAMES WEBPAGE!

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were bornt.
The following are Ghanian day names:

s/No DAY MALE FEMALE
1 Sunday Kwasi Akosua
2 Monday Kwadwo Adwoa
3 Tuesday Kwabena Abenaa
4 Wednesday Kwaku Akua
5 Thursday Yaw Yaa
6 Friday Kofi Afua
7 Saturday Kwame Ama
